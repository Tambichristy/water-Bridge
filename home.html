A drought is a prolonged period
of abnormal low rainfall that
leads to water shortage

there exist various types of droughts
1.Meteroogical droughts
2.Hydrological droughts
3.Agricultural droughts
4.Socioeconomic droughts


Agricultural droughts


occurs when there is
enough moisture in
the soil to sustain
the growth of crops

Meteroogical droughts


they are region specific
they occur when an area
receives less rainfall
than it normally should

Hydrological droughts


occurs when there is
a lack of surface and
subsurface water supply

Socioeconomicdrought


occurs when the water supply
is too low to support humans
and environmental needs
